Alcomancy

A magical drinking game from the creators of PubQuest

About the Game

You are an Alcomancer, a spell-caster in a fantasy realm. Drinking alcohol generates Mana, a special arcane substance which powers your magic. To win, you must be the last Alcomancer standing, by attacking your oponents and depleting their Mana pool. Alcomancers without Mana are defeated, and must take a long rest to recover. Alcomancy takes a stout heart, a stouter mind, and, sometimes, an ice-cold stout.

  • Each Alcomancer begins with 10 Mana points. Each Alcomancer must be responsible for tracking their own Mana pool.
  • Alcomancers take turns casting spells, by drawing a card at random or rolling a 12-sided die.
  • Each spell will specify the following information:
    • Mana Cost. This is the amount of Mana points it takes to activate the spell and its effects.
    • Counterspell. This explains how any Alcomancer -- including the original caster -- can cancel out the spell before its effects happen.
    • Effects. This describes what the spell does.
  • Alternatively, an Alcomancer may opt to add a Mana point to their Mana pool (instead of casting a spell) by taking a sip of alcohol. An Alcomancer may only add a single Mana point in this manner, no matter their drink or size of their sip.

Rules of the Realm

No matter where your adventure takes you, Alcomancers must always abide by the Rules of the Realm.

  • The instant an Alcomancer's Mana pool drops to zero or fewer Mana points, the Alcomancer has been defeated and is out of the game.
  • Fumbling -- any Alcomancer that spills, stumbles, loses something, or bumps into stuff must lose 1 Mana point per infraction.
  • Responsibility -- Alcomancy must be practiced responsibly; this game is intended to be a fun accompaniament to drinking, and not result in anyone suffering a medical emergency.
